03The one caveat, logged
Temperature out of specification · 91 events

The 98°C denaturation setpoint sits just 1°C under the ODTC's rated 99°C block maximum. On the aggressive ramp into denaturation the block overshoots and grazes that ceiling — the 5‑second trace caught a 99.04°C peak, and the device's own faster internal monitor logged 91 "temperature out of specification" events across the 30 cycles, roughly three per cycle. The method completed regardless; these are warnings, not faults, and denaturation at 98–99°C is biologically fine.

Recommended before a real sample run: drop the denaturation setpoint to 97°C, or soften the overshoot parameters into that step, to keep the block off the ceiling. Either change is one line in odtc_protocols.py and re‑checkable with this same report.
