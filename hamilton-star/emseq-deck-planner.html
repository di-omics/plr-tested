4   Run posture

Deck preflight
--mode deck assigns and prints the deck. No motion, no pipetting.
Local sim
--sim-lh runs the 14 liquid-handling legs on the STAR chatterbox. iSWAP and ODTC print as notes.
Motion rehearsal
--confirm RUN_EMSEQ_ODTC_FULL drives real iSWAP motion on empty, water labware. Tips returned; ODTC not heated. Human at the E-stop.
Wet run
Blocked pending qualification: dispense heights, on-deck mixing, SPRI eluate transfer and dry timings, and the ODTC iSWAP geometry.

Everything above is modeled in simulation and has not been run on the instrument. Physical execution stays attended, one process per instrument, with a hand near the E-stop.
